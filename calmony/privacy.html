Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Calmony ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop calendar application ("App").

By using Calmony, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Purpose Storage
Calendar Account Credentials OAuth tokens for Google, Microsoft, and iCloud calendar access Local device only (encrypted)
Calendar Events Display and manage your calendar events Synced with your calendar providers
App Preferences Personalize your experience Local device only

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Purpose Storage
Error Reports Identify and fix bugs (via Sentry) Sentry servers (anonymized)
Usage Analytics Improve app features (via Google Analytics) Google servers (anonymized)
Purchase History Verify subscription status Local device + App Store

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Email content or attachments
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or media files

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Provide Core Functionality
    • Sync and display calendar events from multiple providers
    • Process natural language input for event creation
    • Manage your subscription status
  2. Improve Our Services
    • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
    • Identify and fix technical issues
    • Develop new functionality
  3. Process Transactions
    • Verify in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit
    • Manage subscription renewals and restorations

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted using system keychain
  • No data is transmitted to our servers

5.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for sensitive data
  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • No plain-text storage of credentials

5.3 Data Retention

  • App data is retained until you uninstall the app
  • Analytics data is retained according to third-party service policies
  • Purchase records are maintained by Apple

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

6.1 Access and Control

  • View: Access all your data within the app
  • Export: Export calendar data through your calendar providers
  • Delete: Remove all local data by uninstalling the app

6.2 Data Deletion Request

You can request deletion of your data in the following ways:

  • Local Data: Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data including preferences, cached calendar data, and OAuth tokens.
  • Analytics Data: To request deletion of anonymized analytics data collected by third-party services, please contact us at kcdmlry@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  • Purchase Records: Subscription and purchase data is managed by Apple. To manage your subscription, go to System Settings > Apple ID > Media & Purchases > Manage Subscriptions.

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and confirm completion via email.

6.3 Account Disconnection

You can disconnect calendar accounts at any time:

  1. Go to Settings > Accounts
  2. Select the account to remove
  3. Click "Disconnect"

6.4 Analytics Opt-Out

To disable analytics collection:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy
  2. Toggle off "Send Analytics"

7. Children's Privacy

Calmony is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

If you use Calmony outside of your country of residence, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries where our third-party service providers operate.
