Calmony

All Your Calendars, One Place

A unified calendar app for macOS that brings Google, iCloud, and Microsoft calendars together in one beautiful interface.

  • ⏳
    "My calendar sync is always delayed" Apple Calendar takes forever to sync Google events. You miss meetings because of sync delays.
  • 💸
    "$60/year just for a calendar app?" Premium calendar apps charge subscription fees that add up over time.
  • ⌨️
    "Why can't I just type 'Meeting tomorrow 3pm'?" Most calendar apps make you click through multiple fields to create a simple event.
  • 📋
    "My calendar and reminders are in separate apps" You want to see your schedule and to-do list together, but they're always split apart.

Calmony Solves These Problems

All Calendars in One View

Connect Google, iCloud, and Microsoft Exchange accounts seamlessly. See all your events in a single, unified interface. No more tab switching or app juggling.

Natural Language Quick Add

Just type "Team meeting tomorrow 3pm" or "다음주 월요일 오전 10시 프레젠테이션" and Calmony understands. Create events in seconds, not minutes.

Time Analytics Built-in

Understand how you spend your time. Categorize events and get insights into your work-life balance. Make better decisions about your schedule.

Desktop Widget

Quick glance at your upcoming events right from your desktop. Stay on top of your schedule without opening the full app.
