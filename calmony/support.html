Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my calendar?

  1. Open Calmony
  2. Go to Settings → Accounts
  3. Click Add Account
  4. Select your calendar provider (Google, iCloud, or Microsoft)
  5. Sign in and authorize access

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple:

  1. Open System Settings on your Mac
  2. Click your Apple ID at the top
  3. Select Media & Purchases → Manage
  4. Find Calmony and click Cancel Subscription

Or visit: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Calmony purchase
  4. Select "Request a refund"

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

  1. Open Calmony
  2. Go to Settings → Subscription
  3. Click Restore Purchases
  4. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase

My calendar events are not syncing

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Go to Settings → Accounts
  3. Click Refresh on the affected account
  4. If the issue persists, disconnect and reconnect the account

How do I delete my data?

Calmony stores all data locally on your device. To delete:

  1. Disconnect all calendar accounts in Settings → Accounts
  2. Uninstall the app

This removes all local data. Your calendar events remain safe with your calendar providers.
