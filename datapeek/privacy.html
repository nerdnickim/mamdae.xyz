Privacy Policy

DataPeek - Local Database Browser

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

DataPeek is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your data never leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your database files or their contents
  • SQL queries you execute
  • File names or paths
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

How DataPeek Works

DataPeek operates entirely on your local device:

  • All file processing happens locally
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No account or sign-in required
  • No cloud storage or sync features

Data Storage

DataPeek may store the following locally on your device:

  • Application preferences - Theme, window size, recent files list
  • Query history - Your recent SQL queries (stored locally only)
  • Purchase status - Pro upgrade verification via App Store

This data is stored only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

DataPeek uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store - For app distribution and in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to purchase transactions.

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Children's Privacy

DataPeek does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

Your Rights

Since DataPeek does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. All application data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.
