Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my calendar accounts?

Go to Settings > Accounts > Add Account and select your calendar provider (Google, iCloud, or Microsoft).

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple:

  1. Open Settings on your Mac
  2. Click your Apple ID
  3. Click Media & Purchases
  4. Click Manage under Subscriptions
  5. Find Unical and click Cancel

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

My calendar events aren't syncing

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Go to Settings > Accounts and verify your account is connected
  3. Try disconnecting and reconnecting the account
  4. Restart the app
