Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Unical does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All your data stays on your device and syncs directly with third-party services (Google, Apple, Microsoft) that you choose to connect.

2. Third-Party Services

When you connect external accounts, data is transmitted directly between your device and those services:

  • Google Calendar - governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple iCloud - governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Exchange - governed by Microsoft's Privacy Policy

3. Local Storage

We store the following data locally on your device:

  • OAuth tokens (encrypted in system keychain)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Cached calendar data for offline access

4. Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally and includes:

  • App version and OS version
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Crash reports

5. Data Security

All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption. OAuth tokens are stored in your system's secure keychain.

6. Children's Privacy

Unical is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@mamdae.xyz
